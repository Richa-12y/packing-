2.Take two printout of premailer Hard Copy(Autogenerated mail Instruction for RMA)
3.After that put premailer inside the cardboard Box along with Drive without any Accessories
***If the Drive is Intenal(For the Example like WD BLUE,WD BLACK,WD PURPLE, SSD DRIVE etc...)
The Pack on the **ESD BAG**(Electrostatic Discharge Bags .It is recommended for the protection purpose)
If The drive is External(For the Example like WD MY PASSPORT,WD ELEMENT etc...) Do not need to pack ESD BAG.***
4.Paste the Second one Premailer Hard Copy((Autogenerated mail Instruction for RMA) Outside of the Box
5.Write The 3 Visible Side of the Cardboard Box RMA NUMBER AND DRIVE S/N
6.Inform the Customer(Cx) You have to pay the one side courier Charge
7.Courier The Drive Our Warehouse(WH).
(After Reaching your drive one testing will perfome if you drive is pass the testing

then you get the same drive . If the drive found manipulated then you do not get your drive .)
8.After Reaching drive it will take 10-15 working days to shipped Cx given Address
9.RMA number is valid for 30 days from the date it was issued.
